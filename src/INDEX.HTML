A civic landmark conceived as a folded topographic plate that extends the landscape into the built environment. The building negotiates the dialogue between fjord, forest, and urban fabric — creating thresholds that dissolve the boundary between interior and exterior. Raw concrete, weathered steel, and local timber form a material palette that ages in dialogue with the Nordic climate.
